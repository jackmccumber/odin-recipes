Key Lime Bars

Description

Easy to make and quick to prep, these bars are tasty!

Ingridients

  • Condensed Milk
  • 10 oz lime juice
  • 4 crushed Nature Valley bars
  • Zest of two limes
  • 1 tbsp. butter

Directions

  1. Combine wet ingridients and zest in large bowl and whisk until the the consistency of pancake batter.
  2. Butter baking pan and layer crushed Nature Valley bars.
  3. Slurry lime/milk mixture onto crumbs until covered.
  4. Refridgerate 2 hours before cutting into 4" squares.
  5. Enjoy!
